General Format: [{"front":"word","back":["def1","def2"],"example":["ex1"],"deck":"deck_name","tags":["tag"],"liked":true}]
For Vocabulary: Put the word in "front" and definitions in the "back" array.
For Coding/Tech: Put the question in "front". In the "back" array, write your explanation and use \n for line breaks. You can insert standard Markdown code blocks: \n```javascript\nconsole.log('hi');\n```.
Note: The `deck` property is just a custom text label to help organize cards (e.g., "Programming" or "Spanish").
